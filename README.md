# gcc-trade-flows-analysis

\# GCC Trade Flows Analysis (2015–2023)



An analysis of Gulf Cooperation Council trade patterns, oil dependency,

and economic diversification progress using World Bank and UN Comtrade data.



\## Key Findings



\- \*\*UAE exports only 12% oil\*\* vs Kuwait at 92% — the most diversified GCC economy

\- \*\*Saudi non-oil exports grew +114%\*\* (2015–2023) — Vision 2030 showing results

\- \*\*2022 record exports of $1,590B\*\* were oil-price-driven, masking real diversification

\- \*\*China dominates\*\* as top trade partner for 4 of 6 GCC nations

\- \*\*UAE recovered fastest\*\* from COVID-19 due to its diversified export base
